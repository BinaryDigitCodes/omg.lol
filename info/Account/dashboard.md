# Dashboard

When you log into your account, you land on your Dashboard. The Dashboard is the place where you can see important information at a glance, as well as easily access everything within omg.lol. It’s comprised of large, friendly buttons—everything on it is a giant button.

The very first thing you’ll see is your address (or addresses, if you have more than one). You can click into your address to access its features.
